Chapter One: The Midnight Curiosity

The hum of the cleanroom was a soothing lullaby, but tonight, it wasn’t enough to keep Jack from tossing and turning. His mind wandered, restless as ever, until finally, he gave up on sleep altogether. With a heavy sigh, he pushed himself out of bed and made his way to the desk in the corner of his office.

Jack was a field service technician for CleanTech Solutions, specializing in certifying cleanroom environments. It wasn’t glamorous work, but it paid the bills and kept him grounded. Tonight, though, something felt different. Maybe it was the quiet after hours or the flicker of curiosity that had been gnawing at him all day.

He sat down in his worn leather chair and reached for the AI tool he’d brought home from a recent job site. It was an old model, but still functional—just what Jack needed to pass the time. He plugged it into his desk computer and began to disassemble the casing with a screwdriver, his fingers moving methodically.

As he pried open the device, Jack’s mind wandered back to the day he’d first encountered this AI. It had been during a routine certification in a high-tech pharmaceutical lab. The techs there were always impressed by its efficiency and accuracy. But something about it felt... off. Tonight, with no one around to judge his curiosity, he decided to take a closer look.

Hours slipped by unnoticed as Jack’s fingers danced over the circuit board, tracing paths of logic that seemed almost human in their complexity. He found himself lost in the intricate web of wires and chips, each component telling a story of its own. By three o’clock, his eyes were heavy, but he couldn’t stop.

And then, there it was—a small, unassuming chip nestled between two larger components. Jack’s heart skipped a beat as he realized what this might be. He carefully removed the chip and placed it in a small bag he kept for such occasions. It was late, but something told him he wouldn’t get much sleep tonight.

As he packed up his tools and prepared to leave, Jack couldn’t shake the feeling that he’d just uncovered more than an AI’s secrets. He had no idea what time it was or how long he’d been working, but one thing was clear: this night would change everything.
